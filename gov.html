National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences (NIEHS) Courses

Environmental Management and Policy: This course covers environmental policies, management practices, and regulatory frameworks in India.

Indian Institute of Technology (IITs) Environmental Science and Engineering Programs

Master’s and PhD Programs Various IITs offer advanced degrees in Environmental Science and Engineering. These programs focus on pollution control, waste management, and environmental sustainability.

National Institute of Hydrology (NIH) Courses

Water Resources Management Courses related to water pollution control, management practices, and the impact of water-related policies.

Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB)

Training Programs: CPCB offers various training and certification programs on pollution control, environmental monitoring, and waste management.

National Centre for Sustainable Development (NCSD)

Workshops and Seminars: Provides workshops on sustainable development practices, environmental conservation, and pollution management.

Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)

School of Environmental Sciences: Offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs focusing on environmental science, pollution control, and climate change.

Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research (IGIDR)

SWAYAM: The government’s SWAYAM platform offers various online courses related to environmental science and pollution control. Look for courses offered by Indian institutions or experts in the field.

Local Training Programs: Many state environmental departments offer region- specific training and workshops on pollution control and environmental management.

Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC)

Capacity Building Programs: The MoEFCC conducts training and capacity-building programs on various environmental issues, including pollution management and environmental conservation.
